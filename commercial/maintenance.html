Maintenance

For the best system performance and energy efficiency, we recommend scheduled maintenance twice-yearly for residential, and four times a year for commercial. Our service department offers the following programs for your new heating and air conditioning system…

Spring

  • Check operating pressures
  • Check refrigerant charge
  • Clean or replace filter (standard type)
  • Check compressor
  • Check fan motor
  • Check capacitors
  • Check belts
  • Check coils
  • Check wiring
  • Lubricate motors
  • Check blower motor
  • Check thermostat

Fall

  • Check burner and pilot assemblies
  • Check for cracks in the heat exchanger
  • Check the pilot thermocouple
  • Clean or replace the filter (standard type)
  • Check electronic ignition
  • Check fan operation
  • Check limit switch
  • Check burner adjustment
  • Check temperature rise
  • Check heat anticipator
  • Check and adjust belts
  • Check draft diverter
  • Lubricate motors
  • Check for carbon monoxide
